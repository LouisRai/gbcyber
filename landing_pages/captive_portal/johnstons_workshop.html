Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What does the workshop include?

All workshop prices include instruction and everything you need to complete the project of your choice, we also include hot apple cider and cookies! No need to bring anything other than your creativity and Christmas cheer!! (however we do not provide gloves)

What do I wear?

Our workshops are held in the greenhouse which is not heated. The temperature will be warmer inside, but if the sun is not out, it may feel cooler or damp. We recommend wearing layers, and you may want to bring garden gloves if you have them or mini mitts.

What happens if I’m late or forgot I signed up?

If you're running late, call the greenhouse, and we will catch you up when you arrive. If you forget about your workshop, we'll try to add you to the next available one, but we ask for flexibility.

Can I make a second item in the workshop I signed up for?

You can make a second item if time permits. It’s best to ask when registering, and our staff will prepare a second kit for you to take home.

Can I be refunded for a workshop I can no longer attend?

Workshops are non-refundable. However, we can prepare a kit for pick-up or make the project for you, and you can collect it later. You may also send a friend in your place.

Can my friend join last minute?

We will happily accommodate last-minute guests as long as there is space and materials available for the specific workshop.

What if I have another commitment overlapping the workshop?

Let us know at the start of the workshop if you need to leave early, and we’ll do our best to help. However, we encourage you to relax and enjoy the festive atmosphere!

Can I bring my own decorations and get a discount?

You are welcome to bring your own items, but unfortunately, we cannot offer a discount as the kits and materials were pre-ordered. You can reuse items in future projects.

Why are there fewer plastic decorations?

We are reducing plastic use in our workshops to help the environment. We’re using biodegradable materials wherever possible, including floral foam. We encourage you to pass along old decorations to friends or reuse them.
